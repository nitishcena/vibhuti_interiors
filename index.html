Who We Are

Designing Spaces That Tell Your Story

At Vibhuti Interiors, we believe every space has a story waiting to be told. Based in the heart of Raichur, Karnataka, our studio brings together a passionate team of architects, interior designers, and craftsmen who share one vision — creating interiors that are both breathtaking and functional.

From luxurious bedrooms with bespoke false ceilings to state-of-the-art modular kitchens, every project we undertake is a testament to our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and relentless pursuit of design perfection.
